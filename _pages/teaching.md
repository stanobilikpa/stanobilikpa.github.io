## Teaching Philosophy
My teaching philosophy is grounded in <span style="color:var(--global-theme-color);">experiential learning</span>, <span style="color:var(--global-theme-color);">inclusive pedagogy</span>, and <span style="color:var(--global-theme-color);">research-led instruction</span>. I aim to equip students with strong analytical foundations while fostering creativity, teamwork, and problem-solving skills essential for modern engineering practice.
