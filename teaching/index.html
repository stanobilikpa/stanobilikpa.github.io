Teaching

Courses taught and supported across undergraduate and postgraduate levels in robotics, control, and mechatronics.

I have over eight years of experience delivering and supporting high-quality teaching in robotics, control engineering, mechatronics, automation, and systems engineering across undergraduate and postgraduate programmes. My teaching emphasizes hands-on learning, project-based instruction, and the integration of theory with real-world engineering applications.

Teaching Philosophy

My teaching philosophy is grounded in experiential learning, inclusive pedagogy, and research-led instruction. I aim to equip students with strong analytical foundations while fostering creativity, teamwork, and problem-solving skills essential for modern engineering practice.
